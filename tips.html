Weather Tips

Image Description

In hot weather, stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water and wear light, breathable clothing to stay cool. Protect yourself from the sun by wearing a hat and using sunscreen, and plan outdoor activities for cooler times of the day.(summer)

In cold weather, dress in layers to stay warm, and wear a hat, gloves, and scarf to protect yourself from the cold. Use moisturizer to prevent dry skin, and be aware of signs of hypothermia and frostbite.(winter)

In rainy weather, pack a waterproof jacket and shoes, and carry an umbrella or waterproof cover for your belongings. Be cautious of slippery surfaces and plan indoor activities for heavy rain.

In snowy weather, dress warmly in waterproof, insulated clothing and wear boots with good traction. Use sunglasses to protect your eyes from glare off the snow, and be cautious of icy patches.(winter)

In windy weather, it's important to dress warmly in layers to protect yourself from the wind chill. Use a scarf or face mask to cover your face and prevent windburn. Secure loose items that could be blown away, and be cautious of falling branches or debris.

In extreme weather conditions, stay informed about weather forecasts and follow local advisories and guidelines. Have a plan in place for emergencies, including shelter and communication with others.(summer)
